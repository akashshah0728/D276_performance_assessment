My Career Goals

Direction, motivation, and a sense of purpose are all things you stand to gain by setting clear goals in your professional journey. Often times we can get caught up in the monotony of cyclical lives and not living to our highest potential. Having a set of goals can help focus your time and efforts to opportunities that align with your aspirations. They can also hold you accountable by being able to measure your progress and something clearly defined.

Personally, I would like to earn my B.S in Computer Science by June 2026 so that I can get started on pivoting my career towards software development. Ultimately, I aspire to earn a position at a large video game developer, so I can merge my personal passion with my professional journey.
